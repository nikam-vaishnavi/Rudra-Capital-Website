Our Story

Founded on a Simple Belief

Rudra Capital was founded with one conviction: every individual deserves expert financial guidance — not just those with large portfolios or institutional backing.

We began as a boutique financial practice focused on helping salaried professionals and families navigate the complexity of mutual funds and long-term financial planning. Over the years, that circle of trust has expanded — but our principles have not changed.

Today, Rudra Capital is a registered Mutual Fund Distributor (AMFI) with a growing client base across Maharashtra, offering personalised services across mutual funds, SIP, retirement planning, child education planning, and insurance.

Mutual Funds

Mutual funds pool capital from multiple investors to build diversified portfolios managed by professional fund managers. We help you identify funds that align precisely with your risk tolerance, time horizon, and financial goals.

  • —Risk-profiling and fund selection
  • —Equity, debt, hybrid, and ELSS options
  • —Diversification across sectors and asset classes
  • —Transparent, regulated investment vehicles
  • —Easy online onboarding and portfolio tracking
🔄
Discipline that compounds into wealth

Systematic Investment Plan (SIP)

SIP is the most effective way to build wealth over time — investing fixed amounts at regular intervals to remove market-timing anxiety and harness the full power of compounding. Even modest monthly amounts become significant corpus over 10–15 years.

  • —Start with as little as ₹500/month
  • —Rupee cost averaging reduces risk
  • —Automated, stress-free investing
  • —Flexible — pause, modify, or stop anytime
  • —Goal-linked SIP planning

Securing tomorrow's opportunities today

Child Education Planning

Education costs are rising at 10–12% annually. Without a dedicated plan, this can become a serious financial burden. We create goal-specific investment strategies that account for your child's age, the target institution, and realistic cost projections.

  • —Accurate future cost estimation
  • —Age-appropriate investment strategy
  • —Milestone-based review and adjustment
  • —Protection cover for the earning parent
  • —Tax-efficient vehicle selection

Retirement Planning

Retirement is the longest financial goal and the most unforgiving if underplanned. We help you determine your retirement corpus, assess your current trajectory, and build a structured plan that funds a comfortable, independent retirement.

  • —Inflation-adjusted income projection
  • —National Pension Scheme (NPS) advisory
  • —Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP) setup
  • —Estate and succession considerations
